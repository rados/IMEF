About IMEF

The foundation supports independent media in Bulgaria and South-Eastern Europe, with a focus on empowering media markets on the Balkans.

We are committed to fostering a vibrant ecosystem where independent journalism can thrive.

Our Mission

The Independent Media Entrepreneurship Foundation is dedicated to promoting independent journalism and media literacy. We believe that a well-informed society is the backbone of democracy, and we strive to empower voices that need to be heard.
